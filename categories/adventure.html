Corn Tycoon

Corn Tycoon is the ultimate farm adventure where you can grow, harvest, and sell delicious corn to become the richest farmer in town! Start by clicking on your fields to plant seeds, water them, and harvest corn. As you grow more crops, you can buy handy machines like seeders, irrigators, and harvesters to do the work faster. Don’t forget to upgrade your silos to store more corn and your water tanks to keep your fields nice and green. The more corn you grow, the more money you make! But that’s not all; Corn Tycoon is full of ways to boost your farm and earn even faster! Upgrade your trucks to transport more corn and sell it quicker at the market. Keep an eye on the sky for the flying piggy bank—click it as fast as you can to get a big money boost! As you harvest more and more corn, you’ll be able to prestige, restarting from scratch but unlocking much more expensive corn! Visit the lab to discover awesome upgrades that make your corn worth more, speed up your machines, and even help your soil grow crops quicker. Your farm becomes bigger, faster, and more profitable with every upgrade. Can you become the ultimate Corn Tycoon and grow the biggest corn empire ever? Get ready to click, plant, and harvest your way to farming fame. Let the corn adventure begin!
