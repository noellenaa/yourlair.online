Monster Survivors

Survive endless waves of monsters in this thrilling action-packed adventure!

Game Overview

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last against increasingly challenging waves of monsters. Choose your abilities wisely and upgrade your character to become stronger as you face tougher enemies.

Key Features

  • Endless survival gameplay
  • Multiple character abilities
  • Progressive difficulty system
  • Strategic upgrade choices
  • Various monster types

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Survive as long as possible while collecting experience points to level up and choose new abilities. Defeat monsters to earn points and try to achieve the highest score possible!
